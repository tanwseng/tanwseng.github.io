Professional Summary

Peter is an Adult Educator in the areas of security, both physical and information. He has designed training programs (WSQ and non-WSQ) and consultancy solutions for SMEs and MNCs. With expertise in Project Management and Finance, he is able to value-add to organisations besides training solutions. He is also an adjunct lecturer with Temasek Polytechnic, and course developer cum facilitator with Security Industry Institute.

Skills

Peter is a lifelong learner and has attained qualifications in WSQ Diploma in Security Management, ACTA, DACE, QISP, PMP and MSc Finance. He is currently pursuing qualifications in WSQ Specialist Diploma in Educational Quality Management and CISSP.
